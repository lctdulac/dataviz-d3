France Power consumption and CO2 calendar



This visualisation shows the electricity consumptions and the CO2 emissions in France, day after day, as a Heat Map.

  • The data comes from data.gouv.fr, the French government open-data platform.
  • The data is recorded by RTE, the distribution department of the national electricity company EDF.
  • The data is recorded every 15 minutes, but the yearly data are limited to 2012, 2013 and 2014.
