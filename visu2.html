French power consumption and CO2 calendar



This visualisation either shows the electricity consumption or the CO2 emissions in France, day after day, as a Heat Map.

  • The data comes from data.gouv.fr, the French government open-data platform.
  • The data is recorded by RTE, the company in charge of the French power grid.
  • It is recorded every 15 minutes, but the yearly data are only available from 2012 to 2014.
