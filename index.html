Electricity in France - Consumption, CO2 footprint and Energy mix.

This websites aims at clarifying how the energy is produced and used in France.

About this website

This project tries to answer to the following questions using data-visualization:

  • During the day, when is the power consumed ?
  • When does the energy production release the most CO2 ?
  • Where does the electricty come from ?
