Daily consumption and energy mix



This barchart shows the daily evolution of power production.
The first chart includes nuclear energy and the second doesn't.

  • The data comes from data.gouv.fr, the French government open-data platform.
  • The data is recorded by RTE, the company in charge of the French power grid.
  • The chart represents the average hourly production in MWh during the day between 12/01/2019 and 02/12/2020.
